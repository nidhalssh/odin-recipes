Pizza

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough and let it rise
  2. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C)
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface
  4. Spread tomato sauce over the dough
  5. Add cheese and your favorite toppings
  6. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden

From the Editor

This recipe is versatile; feel free to customize the toppings to your liking!
For a crispier crust, bake on a preheated pizza stone.
